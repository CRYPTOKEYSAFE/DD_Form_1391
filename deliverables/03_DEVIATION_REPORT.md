## Portfolio-wide deviations (apply to all five buildings)

1. **UNIFORMAT removed from Block 9 face**: Manual §3.1 (p. 19) requires line-item names by facility / category code, not by UNIFORMAT II. UNIFORMAT continues to live in the SCOPE_DETAIL tab of the cost estimate workbook for internal traceability.

2. **Discipline-first naming removed**: The Primary Facility line names the facility, not the discipline. Manual §3.1 (p. 19), §1.7.1 (p. 11). Block 10 PS statements describe the work in plain language.

3. **LSH repositioned**: from a free-standing line between Contingency and Design (Marine Corps FSRM workbook convention) to a Special Construction Features sub-line under Supporting Facilities (manual §3.1 supporting item 1, §4.9 (p. 30)). Manual is silent on LSH; placement rationale documented in deliverable 01.

4. **Single Primary Facility line per building**: Each building gets ONE Primary Facility line at the building-name level (not multiple lines for each scope group). Conversion/Alteration scope is described in Block 10 PS, not enumerated on the Block 9 face. Manual §3.7 (p. 23) and §1.7.1 (p. 11).

5. **Cost rollup labels match AF exemplar conventions**: SUBTOTAL, CONTINGENCY (X.X%), TOTAL CONTRACT COST, SUPERVISION INSPECTION AND OVERHEAD (X.X%), DESIGN COST, CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT, TOTAL FUNDED COST, DESIGN/BUILD - DESIGN COST, TOTAL PROJECT COST, TOTAL PROJECT COST (ROUNDED), EQUIPMENT FROM OTHER APPROPRIATIONS (NON-ADD).

6. **Total Funded vs Total Project distinction preserved**: MC FSRM convention shows two totals (Total Funded = construction-only, then DB Design adds to Total Project). AF exemplars show one (Total Request, with DB Design baked in). Both are visible in the deliverable.

7. **Dollar values preserved exactly**: Every reported line value matches the CEPBKUP workbook to the dollar. Where the PAX .md disagreed with the workbook, the workbook governed .
