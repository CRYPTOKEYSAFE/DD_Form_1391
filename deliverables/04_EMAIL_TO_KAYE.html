If you would prefer it folded into the Primary Facility line cost with the breakout in the Block 10 PS statement, the dollar value does not change. Either home works.
  • SIOH 8.0%. OCONUS FSRM customer-directed for MCIPAC G-F. Documented client deviation from the standard 7.3% OCONUS rate per DASD-Con. Applies to all five buildings.
